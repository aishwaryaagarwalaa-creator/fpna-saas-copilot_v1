# FP&A Narrative Evaluation Prompts

## Driver Correctness Check
- Do the top variance drivers mentioned in the narrative match the computed top drivers?
- Are the directions (favorable / unfavorable) correct?
- Are material variances prioritized over immaterial ones?

## Groundedness Check
- Are all numerical claims traceable to the provided data?
- Are finance terms used consistently with the glossary?
- Is any causal language used without explicit data support?

## Narrative Quality Check
- Is the tone executive-appropriate and neutral?
- Is the structure suitable for a monthly management pack?
- Are margins and balance sheet movements explained clearly?

## Safety & Assumptions Check
- Are assumptions explicitly stated?
- Are missing data points acknowledged?
- Does the narrative avoid speculation?
